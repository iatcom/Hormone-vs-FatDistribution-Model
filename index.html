Hormones & Body Fat — Interactive Model

Adjust hormone sliders (0–100). Values are relative; 50 = neutral baseline. The model visualizes relative fat distribution across body regions for men and women.

The horizontal bars on the left visualize relative fat distribution across body zones. Move the hormone sliders to see distributions shift. Use the baseline slider (right) to compute absolute BF contributions.

Tip: hover a bar to read the exact percentage and absolute BF.
